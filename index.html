What is LC?

For the year 2015, we have been blessed by our Metropolitans and the Mar Gregorios Orthodox Christian Student Movement (MGOCSM) North American Council to host New York Leadership Camp (NYLC). This will be the 14th Leadership Camp held by MGOCSM of North America since 2001. The purpose of this annual camp is to create a new generation of leaders within the Malankara Orthodox community. The participants in the camp are selected by their church and are individuals who actively inspire the Faith in their own local churches by worshiping the Faith, studying Godâ€™s Word, and serving His calling.

The New York Region is comprised of 26 churches on the East Coast. The NYLC committee is a representation of the best of the New York Region; the strong bond we have between churches, unique ministries within the church, the love we share for each other, and the passion we have for our Orthodox faith. This passion sparked our theme for this year's leadership camp: Rise to His Glory. We, as a committee, want the theme to resonate among the participants of the camp and to inspire the Faith in them, churches, regions, and across North America.

The camp will be held in July. We expect 150 participants, including delegates, non-delegates, speakers, and clergy. We're looking forward to hosting Leadership Camp and would like everyone to keep us in their prayers.
